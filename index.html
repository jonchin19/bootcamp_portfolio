I love learning about people and designing experiences that help people finish their work quickly and efficiently. My professional interests include leading product strategy, creating effective product teams, and mentoring early-career designers.

Outside of the UX world, I can be found coaching high school football, learning a new sport, and volunteering at the local library, theater, or animal rescue.
